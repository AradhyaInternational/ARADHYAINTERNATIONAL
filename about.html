MANUFACTURING

We manufacture all the items in our Saharanpur based manufacturing unit and also customise the products as per the requirement of the consumer. Skilled labourers and perfect Design Engineers are indulged to craft creativity in our unit.
Various types of Yoga Bags (made with Drill Cotton Fabric andNon Woven Fabric) and Yoga T-Shirts are also customised so that it can provide a branding support to our prestigious Yoga Centre consumers and help them to maintain their Best in Class services.
Manufactured items are processed most carefully with our trusted and reliable logistics partners. This ensure on time delivery and proper handling of products, which is our topmost priority.
